bob's story

Wet Oar Rafting has been tackling the toughest rapids since 1969, when Bob McBobson was inspired by his love of the water. He wanted to share his passions with the world, and the best way to do that was through tours! Wanting the most authentic experience possible, Bob searched for years for the perfect river to host his tours. That’s when he finally found the perfect section of the Kiwetinok river. And ever since then, he has been bringing the joys of deep-forest rafting to those seeking a new kind of adventure!
Some say Bob chose this location, but Bob insists that The River chose him.
