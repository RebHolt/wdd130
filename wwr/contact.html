Frequently Asked Questions:

Do I need to bring anything?

Bring a water bottle and lunch, weather appropriate clothing, a coat, and an orange lifejacket. We'll provide everything else!

Why are you so far away?

We pride ourselves on our unique rafting experience! No other company can make you feel so close to nature while still feeling safe and sound.

What if I get lost in the woods?

You won't.

Will the trip be cancelled in case of bad weather?

Absolutly not! Rain or shine, snow or apocalyptic event, we stick to our promise to give you the best experience of your life!
